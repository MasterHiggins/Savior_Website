Full release 1.1.0
Content Additions:
Added the Mesa:
-Slightly smaller than the Empire
-Contains two new enemies
-Contains a new story mechanic
Added the Wizard:
-Appears around the world in various spots
-Delivers lore on what the prophecy and the world really could be
Added Farming Refunds:
-Farming perks can now be refunded
-For perks that have multiple levels, you are only refunded once

Gameplay Tweaks:
Farm statues now have dialogue after purchasing a perk
The cutscene that plays after defeating the boss in the mountain has been adjusted
Opening cutscene has been adjusted Slightly
Added a note on saving to the pause screen

Bug Fixes:
Addressed multiple controller issues:
-Some controllers had control issues that have since been addressed
Several Farming Issues:
-Rounding errors for speed
-One statue accounting for the wrong perk unlocked
-Some statues occasionally not displaying text in very niche circumstances

Known Issues:
Some controllers may still face problems. If you do, please contact the developer and state what controller you had the issue with

Additional Notes:
This update focuses a lot on content. Bug fix 1.1.1 is already underway and expected to roll out soon.

For Developers:
This build does not have dev controls enabled. Those who are permitted to work on the game should already have their dev builds.
