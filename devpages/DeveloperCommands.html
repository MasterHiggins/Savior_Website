Devloper Commands

Please use with caution on your distributed development build

To activate these developer console commands, press 9 on the keyboard. Ensure the level editor is not on first.

ReloadArea

Reloads the area as if it was just generated. Enemy generation takes a second to catch up with this command.

SaveState

Force saves the game.

LoadState

Loads the last saved game.

SpellsToggle

Toggles on and off spells.

CurrentQuestLevel

Input an integer afterwards to change the quest in sequence.

MapChangeLevel

Input an integer afterwards to change the quest in sequence. For the sake of testing, this should almost always be 0.

Area

Input an area id (ex: main:1:1). Follow up with the ReloadArea command.

WeaponLevel

Input an integer that acts as the weapon id. This will change the weapon, however the graphic will be incorrect after the highest supported value.

MaterialCount

Sets the amount of materials you have.

MaxHealth

Sets the max health you can have based on the specified integer. Graphics will not display until area is reloaded, but the change is instant.

Health

Sets your health to the specified integer.

CustomSaveString

DO NOT USE THIS COMMAND UNLESS ABSOLUTELY NECESSARY.
After entering command, you will be prompted and must answer "Yes" to continue. After that, input the string you want to have as your save file. If you follow that up by answering "Yes", then it will be loaded automatically.
