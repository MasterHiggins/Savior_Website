Release Candidate Update

Article from Chris Higgins
(Developer)
This article does not contain spoilers

Hi everyone.

I have a huge update today.

Today, Savior entered the Release Candidate stage. This means I have found no new issues, and the game is now considered finished. For Part one at least.

Wow, that was liberating.

Here's essentially what the release candidate stage means:
No major issues found
A private testing group
Last minute issues fixed
Promotion ramp up
Preorders open

By the end of the month, the 1.0.0 version will be built and ready for release.

Ultimately, I know that issues will be found post release, so from May 23rd, 2026 until the same time in June, I will take a break from Part 2 for weekly bug fix updates.

As for the timeline, Part 2 will begin loose development in the coming weeks, and full development next month.

The game is almost here for release.
