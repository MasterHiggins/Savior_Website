SAVIOR TOMORROW

HOLY F*** WE MADE IT.

Savior comes out in just a couple of hours now. I started work on this project in December of 2023. This year will mark THREE YEARS of development. While the game isn't releasing finished, it's still a huge deal. And now, the narrative begins. While Savior's story is way more outside of just what is told in the game, it's a very satisfying narrative.

I'll be up late tonight until Savior comes out, and making sure everything goes well for the first bit. It will be my birthday by then, and I do have some things to do. That said, I am more excited for Savior's release than I am for those festivities. I've splurged on champagne, I've got plenty of snacks, I'm stoked.

As I've said before, no one is more excited for the game to come out than me, and I'm still pretty adamant on that.

I hope you all like Savior, but I think what I want to hear from more is the group that doesn't like it. That way it can improve. I didn't have a team behind me on this like I have some other projects, so something I might think is cool might not be, or things may need to be improved. Of course, I love feedback, and I hope to hear some.

Only a few hours left.
