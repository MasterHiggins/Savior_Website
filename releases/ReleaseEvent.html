Beta Release Event

Article from Chris Higgins
(Developer)
This article does not contain spoilers

As announced already, Savior will be getting a public beta release this Friday, with a Steam storepage and website domain registered within the next two weeks. However, not a whole lot was mentioned outside of that, so here is a proper outline of what's to come.

Today is Monday, and nothing will really be happening Tuesday either. On Wednesday, there will be a short overview video, going in depth on mechanics, regions, and anything necessary to know. There will also be a trailer or two in this video.

On Thursday, there will be a developer presentation on how the game is being put together. There will not be any new features shown off during the presentation. It will be meant solely as a way to see how the game is being developed.

And then lastly, on Friday the beta will be available to download in the beta tab that has been plaguing this site for months.
