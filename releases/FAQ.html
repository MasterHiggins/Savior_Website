Savior Announcements

Article from Chris Higgins
(Developer)
This article does not contain spoilers

Hello everyone!

Savior (Part 1) is coming out soon. Hopefully everyone is excited for it. There is a small number of wishlists, but I'm happy with the number. It would be great if that number grows even further *cough*.

I expect a few questions before the game releases, so now with shorts releasing often, now is a good time to answer some of what I see to be some frequently asked questions. (If you personally have a question, send a message at LinkedIn).

What is the core story of Savior?

Savior doesn't have much of a story right now, however, Part 2 expands upon it. I would like to highlight that the story focuses on the protagonist, and the relevance of the plot revolves completely around them.

How would you describe the gameplay loop?

The gameplay is something Part 1 heavily focuses on. The world is much more linear than I anticipated, so much so that my prior description of an open world is practically incorrect. Despite that, the game has very open elements, and exploring the lore and taking down enemies until a thrilling story in Part 2 is something I think is great. The loop is really good, and while I think it's great, others (reviews soon) had a lot of fun.

What's with releasing in parts?

Savior is releasing in Parts for a few reasons. First, it's nice to just release it, get it out. Second of all, it feels natural. The game is at a turning point at the spot Part 1 ends where the story really kicks off. Lastly, it allows for feedback, and for essential bugs to be fixed so it doesn't remove the immersion of Part 2. I am not a story teller, but Part 2 ends very well I think, and it would be a shame if bugs got in the way of that.

When will Part 2 release?

Soon. I don't expect it to take another 2 years, sometime in 2027 is a good bet.

How often will updates be between part 1 and 2?

As you may know, I usually do a general development update every season. I expect that to not change, plus it should also include a minor content update.

How will my progress be saved?

Progress is saved both in session and a hard save. The hard save is created whenever you go to the main menu. This is the only way to save your progress for next time you play. The in session save is created when interacting with certain objects and pausing. This is the save that loads when getting a game over, and the most recent one is what the hard save consists of.

When will the first content update after Part 1 be released?

Very shortly after.
