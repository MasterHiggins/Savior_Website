Developer Video Release

Article from Chris Higgins
(Developer)
This article does not contain spoilers

Tomorrow, there will be the release of a new Savior overview video.

In it, there will be details of the game's dungeons, spells, the fifth area, map, and more.

There will also be details on the website, steam page release, as well as a beta build for mac.

If you would like to see the video as soon as it releases, the countdown is now live. Once the countdown ends, you'll be taken to a page with the video, ready to play.

Lastly, I would like to extend a heartfelt thank you for sticking with the game so far. I hope that I can release a product that is fun to play and intriguing to theorize over. If you played the beta, you'll know there is some hidden lore surrounding the Nuggetarian Kingdom that I hope can be fleshed out and enjoyed when the game releases later this year.

Thank you so much.
