Release Change

Article from Chris Higgins
(Developer)
This article does not contain spoilers

Back in July, you may remember an article I wrote where I discussed how Savior was going to go. Well, today, I have some updates. It's both good and bad, depending on how you look at it.

I have decided to release Savior in multiple updates.

So, starting with release dates. The first update is releasing in November. This will be the first instance to the paid game. It will be about the first full 3 areas, so up to spells. Then updates will be released when they are finished. They might be regular, they might not.

The game is taking an incredibly long time to release, so while I still anticipate that the full game will be finished by May 22nd, 2026, I would rather give paying players the ability to play some of the game still.

Because of this change, the Steam Page will be going up in October, and what I anticipate to be a late October. When my first release is ready.

The price will also be different to reflect this. The game will now launch at $9.99 CAD, but I may increase the price later. Currently though, my current plan is to keep it at $9.99. There is no need to be greedy.
