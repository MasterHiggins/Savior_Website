Savior Announcements

Article from Chris Higgins
(Developer)
This article does not contain spoilers

Hello everyone!

Huh, looks like the Spring Update wasn't quite done...

First of all, I would like to thank those who have looked at the Savior Steam Demo.

ALL TWENTY THOUSAND OF YOU???

I do not have words beyond the fact I am blown away. Truly, thank you. That os such a large number, and it ignores views on the main page, the demo installs, wishlists, and more. I was going to celebrate when we hit a certain number of wishlists. that number doubled out of nowhere. I am truly humbled and honoured. Thank you.

At this time, the final rooms of 1.1.0 are being finalized. Once they are done, I will begin work on the side content of the update. It's a bit smaller, but you will love it.

In your head, I may just be a developer, but in practice, I am a person working a whole other job. I actually work in a daycare at a local non profit. You will never believe what a kid said to me today. "Hey Mr? Are you the guy working on Savior?"

I genuinely cannot even begin to fathom that. How? I do this as a hobby, yet it's spilling into my actual life.

I don't know what to say.

Still, I cannot believe the support. I really am proud and on the verge of tears. Even though it's a small number, thank you guys. Truly.
