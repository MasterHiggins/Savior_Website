While trying to meet with your girlfriend, an ancient myth, always referred to as "The Great Ending" began. You have no idea how, but you've ended up on a continent across the world. You need to get back to Melony. You must be her savior.

Explore an open world with enemies, materials, and secrets to find. Listen to the reactions of the new apocalypse. Defeat enemies. Just run around in circles panicking. The world is your's, and there are no limits to your adventure.
